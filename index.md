## Areas of thought

### Research
I am current researching in the area of quantum computation. My interest lies in inventing cool protocols that improves the resource usage of a quantum computer. You can find our more about my research identity in this blog!

### Philosophy
Since the beginning of my undergraduate studies in theoretical physics, it has been in my interest to figure out the status of Eastern philosophy in the modern quantitative world. That naturally leads me to the mind-body problem, and the hard-problem of consciousness. This blog is meant to share the opinions that I have accumulated over the years of what Eastern traditions really say about the mind. 

In the modern days, tremendous progress has been made in the modelling of the consciousness mind through theory like Integrated Information Theory (IIT) and Global Workspace Theory (GWT). This section is also meant to make comments about the two theories, as a reference to both the Eastern and Western traditions.

### Mathematics
Modern mathematics has expanded in an incredible pace since 300 years ago, so much so that there are an massive amount of profound mathematical concepts invented over the years. I believe that there are much to learn from how the giants in mathematics think, by simply reviewing the works. 

This is a place where I write about the topics I have learnt about mathematics, especially in category theory, that I think changes how we view the world as it is.
