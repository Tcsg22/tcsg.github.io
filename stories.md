## Writing, how it all began

Although writing was not in my neighbourhood of interest, somehow I have started writing on things that I have encountered during my journey. 

For example, I believe that we are currently living in a civilisation upgrade (while running out of fossil fuels!), and it is kind of cool to write about this in general. Consider this, it just so happened that, we are born precisely in the early 21 century when globalisation is actually a thing, there are a billions of population walking at every corner on the Earth, and information has become almost too available. And soon, we will be able to fabricate any biological functions through protein folding, travel to the Moon, and watching robot cops roaming on the street. There is a lot to say about this, for any human beings that live precisely at this crossing point of the human history. 

On the other hand, there are much to say, not just about this era, but what it means to be living as a person in a specific cultural upbringing. How much does the wise in our past says are still valid in the present tense, and how much is a tradition still the way it is without the risk of getting commercialised? Anyway, this is the space for this sort of topics that I will unintentionally write about.
