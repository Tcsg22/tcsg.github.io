This poem is inspired by a legendary Buddhist monk in China whose existence is instrumental to the preservation of Chinese Buddhism. The first two lines of the poem is a reference to what he saw in his dream before his passing. Interestingly, the same phasing can be found in the poem of a monk from the Ming dynasty who is said to be the past life of the legendary monk.

碧溪水流絕，佛印橋頭月。松風不答我，但聞寂靜說。
