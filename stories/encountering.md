This poem, written in Chinese, intends to capture the contrast between what happens in reality versus what is perceived, in the event of encountering someone in your life. 

天上流星頃刻， 劃破少年南柯夢。 
人海天數茫茫， 流向落花紅塵中。
廟裏殘花一束，才下心中默默情。
路上行人相送， 卻在燈火闌珊處。
